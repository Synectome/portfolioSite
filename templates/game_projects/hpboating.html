Hyde Park B.I.A. Sailboat Racing

A 3D boat racing game with live leader-board, created in the summer of 2021 for the Hyde Park Business Improvement Association's annual Pond Festival. The game was made in a low-poly style using the Godot game engine, and blender 3D modeling software. This game was released under my newly created Tiny Rhino Games brand.
